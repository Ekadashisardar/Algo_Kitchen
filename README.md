# 🍳 AlgoKitchen – AI Recipe Generator

AlgoKitchen is a **full-stack AI-powered recipe generator** that helps users cook delicious meals using the ingredients they already have at home.

Users can enter available ingredients, and the AI suggests recipes with cooking instructions, ingredients, and nutrition information.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🧠 **AI Recipe Generation** based on user ingredients
* 🥕 **Cook With What You Have** – generate recipes from available ingredients
* 📊 **Ingredient Match Percentage** to show how well a recipe fits your ingredients
* 🥗 **Nutrition Information** (calories per serving)
* 🍽 **Cuisine Tags & Filters** (e.g., Indian, American)
* ⏱ **Cooking Time Estimates**
* ❤️ **Save Recipes to Collection**
* 🧺 **My Pantry** – store ingredients you have at home
* 📄 **Download Recipe as PDF**
